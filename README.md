# Lantern

A short browser-based horror walking game. You carry a lantern down a moonlit
mountain path. Don't let the wick die before you reach the village.

**[Play it →](https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/lantern/)**

## Controls

- **Move** — mouse
- **Flare** — hold left click *(burns oil faster, scares the wisps)*

## How to play

The lantern oil drains as you walk. Glowing wisps drift through the dark — catch
them to refill the wick. They wander unpredictably and bolt away from a flared
light, so you have to time the intercept with the small lantern. As the oil drops,
shadows take notice. If your wick dies, you've got lost in the woods.
